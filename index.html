A note on how this library works

This is a free, non-commercial village library resource, open to every student and family in the community. Class 1–10 and Intermediate resources point to the official Telangana Government portal (SCERT Telangana), where every textbook is free to download — direct per-subject links are being added as they're mapped, so some entries still say "PDF coming soon." Degree PDFs should only be added if you hold the right to redistribute them. Competitive-exam entries are a reading guide, not a file library — the books listed are commercially published and are not distributed here.

All content remains the property of its respective boards, universities, and authors. If you are a rights holder and would like something removed, please reach out below and it will be taken down promptly.
